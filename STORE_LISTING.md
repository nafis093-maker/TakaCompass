## Full description (max 4000 chars)

**EN:**
```
Hisab is a money tracker built for Bangladesh. Log spending in seconds — by voice, from an SMS, or a quick tap — and finally see where your taka goes.

Why people use Hisab:

🎤 ADD BY VOICE (বাংলা too)
Just say it — “৫০০ দুপুরের খাবার”, “bill 1200”, “got salary 50000” — and Hisab fills in the amount, type and category for you.

📩 READ YOUR SMS
Bank, bKash and Nagad transaction texts are turned into ready-to-confirm entries. You tap to approve — nothing is sent anywhere.

📊 SEE WHERE IT GOES
A clean home with your total, this month’s spending by category, savings rate and a “what to do next” insight that surfaces your most important money issue first.

💰 BUDGETS THAT WORK
Set monthly limits per category and watch a simple bar fill up. Get nudged before you overspend.

🕌 ZAKAT CALCULATOR
Pick which wealth counts, set the gold rate, and Hisab estimates your 2.5% above nisab.

📈 PLAN THE BIG STUFF
Emergency fund, DPS/FDR maturities, Sanchayapatra returns, loan EMIs, tax-rebate room and goal planning — tuned to Bangladeshi rates.

✨ HISAB WRAPPED
Your month in money, animated and shareable.

🔒 PRIVATE BY DESIGN
No bank logins. No account required. Your numbers stay on your device — not on our servers. Continue as a guest and start in seconds.

Fully usable in বাংলা or English, with taka (৳) throughout and Bangla numerals.

Hisab — Plan. Track. Grow.
```
